## 💼 Overview

Solution engineering internship at Salesforce: building and demoing GenAI systems for enterprise clients, typically on tight timelines.

## 🔧 What the Role Actually Involved

Each engagement meant understanding a client's workflow, scoping a prototype that fit their infrastructure, and delivering something they could actually run, not just a slides deck. That meant working within real enterprise security constraints, not idealized conditions.

- Built **AI Agents** on the Salesforce Agentforce platform for autonomous task execution.
- Delivered proofs of concept to **5+ large businesses**, adapting to different industries and integration setups.
- Prototyped GenAI workflows for **sales and service automation**: case routing, response generation, pipeline management.

## 🏆 Outcome

5+ enterprise client engagements completed. Several prototypes moved forward into client evaluation.
