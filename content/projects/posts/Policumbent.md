## 🚴 Overview
Work across software engineering and team leadership for Team Policumbent, focused on onboard systems for high-speed streamliners, including Cerberus and Phoenix.

## 🔧 Technical Contributions
- Built Raspberry Pi software interfacing with Garmin ANT+ sensors for real-time biometric data logging.
- Developed an ESP32-based weather station for atmospheric monitoring.
- Implemented MQTT-based data visualization and telemetry workflows.
- Supported systems that enabled streamliners to reach 130 km/h.
- Improved rider performance by 20% on the Cerberus handbike workstream.
