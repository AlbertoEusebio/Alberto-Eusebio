Weather Station (Team Policumbent)

🌡️ Overview IoT weather station developed for Team Policumbent to monitor environmental conditions during high-speed tests and record attempts. 🔧 Technical Scope Built an ESP32-based station to capture wind and atmospheric data. Processed sensor streams to support race-condition assessment and timing decisions. Published telemetry to an MQTT broker for near-real-time monitoring. Connected clients for remote visualization of weather updates. 🎯 Relevance The system supported safer and more informed decision-making when operating streamliners at speeds above 130 km/h.

Streamliners (Team Policumbent)

🚴 Overview Work across software engineering and team leadership for Team Policumbent, focused on onboard systems for high-speed streamliners, including Cerberus and Phoenix. 🔧 Technical Contributions Built Raspberry Pi software interfacing with Garmin ANT+ sensors for real-time biometric data logging. Developed an ESP32-based weather station for atmospheric monitoring. Implemented MQTT-based data visualization and telemetry workflows. Supported systems that enabled streamliners to reach 130 km/h. Improved rider performance by 20% on the Cerberus handbike workstream. 🏆 Leadership and Results 2× IHPVA World Human Powered Speed Challenge Champions (2022 and 2023). Matched the world record in the 2023 edition as Team Leader. Led 9 divisions, 4 vehicles, and 100+ engineering students. Coordinated technical activities with 23 active sponsors. Represented the team at Automation and Testing Turin Fair and Maker Faire Rome.
