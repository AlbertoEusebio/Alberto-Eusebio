Weather Station (Team Policumbent)

ESP32 and MQTT telemetry system for race-condition monitoring in high-speed streamliners
map[email:albertoeusebio72@gmail.com name:Alberto Eusebio]

🌡️ Overview#

IoT weather station developed for Team Policumbent to monitor environmental conditions during high-speed tests and record attempts.

🔧 Technical Scope#

  • Built an ESP32-based station to capture wind and atmospheric data.
  • Processed sensor streams to support race-condition assessment and timing decisions.
  • Published telemetry to an MQTT broker for near-real-time monitoring.
  • Connected clients for remote visualization of weather updates.

🎯 Relevance#

The system supported safer and more informed decision-making when operating streamliners at speeds above 130 km/h.
